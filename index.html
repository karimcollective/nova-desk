Frequently asked questions

Do I need to book in advance for a Day Pass?

It helps, but it's not required. Booking ahead just guarantees your desk during busy weeks.

Can I upgrade from a Day Pass to a membership later?

Yes — we'll credit your most recent day pass toward your first month if you upgrade within 14 days.

Is there a minimum commitment for the Dedicated Desk plan?

No long-term contract. Dedicated Desk and Private Office plans are billed monthly and can be cancelled anytime.
